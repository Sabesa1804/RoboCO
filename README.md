🤖 CodeWorks — An Immersive 3D Computer Science Learning Experience

«Don't just read Computer Science. Walk through it.»

---

🚀 Overview

CodeWorks is a fully immersive 3D interactive web application that transforms traditional computer science education into a futuristic, explorable world.

Instead of reading static notes or watching slides, users enter a robot-powered IT company where every department represents a core area of software engineering. Concepts become physical objects, algorithms become machines, and software architecture becomes a city you can explore.

Learning happens through interaction, experimentation, animation, and discovery.

---

🌍 The Story

Welcome to CodeWorks Industries.

You are a newly recruited software engineer entering one of the world's most advanced technology companies.

The company isn't just a workplace...

It's a living representation of Computer Science.

A central AI guides you through the headquarters, introducing each department before sending you deeper into specialized laboratories where theory comes alive through interactive simulations.

Every room teaches by showing—not telling.

📚 Educational Philosophy

Traditional learning often separates theory from practice.

CodeWorks combines:

- Visual learning
- Interactive exploration
- Real-time experimentation
- Storytelling
- Gamification
- Practical coding

The objective is to transform abstract concepts into memorable experiences.

If you can see it, manipulate it, and experiment with it, you're more likely to understand and retain it.
